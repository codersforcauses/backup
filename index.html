Coders For Causes

Student developers for charities and not for profits

About us

The clubs mission is to promote and facilitate student development projects for charities and not for profits. By doing this we can help student developers gain experience working on real world problems to solve real world solutions, and help deserving causes at the same time.

Committee

Coders for Causes is a brand new organisation, and needs a committee dedicated to helping the club succeed. Here are the roles of committee members.

President

The President is responsible for the overall direction and running of the club, and directs the rest of committee.

Vice President

Responsible for assisting the President where needed.
