Coders For Causes

Student developers for charities and not for profits

About us

The clubs mission is to promote and facilitate student development projects for charities and not for profits. By doing this we can help student developers gain experience working on real world problems to solve real world solutions, and help deserving causes at the same time.

Committee

Coders for Causes is a brand new organisation, and needs a committee dedicated to helping the club succeed. Here are the roles of committee members.

President

The President is responsible for the overall direction and running of the club, and directs the rest of committee.

Vice President

Responsible for assisting the President where needed.

Secretary

Responsible for administration duties within the club, including but not limited to keeping meeting minutes, assisting team organisation and project planning.

Treasurer

Responsible for the clubs finances and financial record keeping.

Diversity officer

As a brand new club, we believe that promoting diversity and inclusivity should be a priority. This role will involve helping to ensure that all student developers are equally aware of the opportunities available to them with the club.

Sponsorship officer

This role will involve searching for and being responsible for corporate partnerships to ensure the success of the club into the future.

Client officer

As client officer your role will be to find client projects for our student teams.

Publicity officer

The club publicity officer will be responsible for ensuring that club messages are received by students, and increasing awareness of the club on campus.
